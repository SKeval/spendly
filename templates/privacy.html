Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and power the features you use. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the app; these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond as promptly as possible.
